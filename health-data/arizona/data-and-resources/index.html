Data Sources

These data sources provide information for communities looking for more local data. These sources provide either unique, local data; more information on demographic breakdowns (e.g., age, sex, race/ethnicity); or data for sub-county geographic units (e.g., cities, zipcodes or school districts).

Name Description Breakdown

Arizona Department of Health Services - Hospital Discharge Data AZ Hospital Compare is a searchable and comparative information tool containing data on hospital care, quality, charges and costs.

Demographic
Arizona Department of Transportation Motor Vehicle Crash Facts The Arizona Department of Transportation Motor Vehicle Crash Facts is an annual statistical review of the motor vehicle crashes in the State of Arizona. The results are compiled from Arizona Traffic Accident Reports submitted to the Arizona Department of Transportation by state, county, city, tribal and other law enforcement agencies.

Arizona Immunization Program The Arizona Department of Health Services provides data on school and childcare immunization coverage, the national immunization survey and the personal beliefs exemptions study.
