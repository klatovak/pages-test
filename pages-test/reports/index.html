RWJF Culture of Health Prize Reports

The Prize was conceived, led and managed by County Health Rankings & Roadmaps at the University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute from 2013-2021. The Prize has honored and elevated U.S. communities working at the forefront of advancing health, opportunity, and equity for all. Reports on Prize-winning communities can provide guidance on how to accelerate efforts to give everyone in your community a fair and just opportunity for health.
